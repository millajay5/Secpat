Welcome to Secpat

Secpat Oil and Gas Ltd is a private company incorporated in 2014 registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) of the Federal Republic of Nigeria under Companies and Allied Matter Act of 1990. The Company is limited by shares with Certificate Number. Secpat Oil and Gas is a subsidiary of BML Interworld Ltd and Ujuaku Industrial Ltd which are located in Uyo, Aba and Owerri respectively. Secpat Oil and Gas is registered to carry out business in the downstream sector of the petroleum industry including importation, storage, retail and distribution of gas and petroleum products such as Liquefied Petroleum Gas (LPG) Compressed Natural Gas (CNG) Premium Motor Spirit (PMS) Dual Purpose Kerosene (DPK) and Automotive Gas Oil (AGO)
